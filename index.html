    EstudioLAAVA was founded in 2016 by Laura de la Vega and Adrian Aranda. Both born and raised in La Habana, Cuba with backgrounds in the Arts. The boundlessness of art awakened a passion at a very early age for creation and collaboration. After graduating from Florida International University and University of Florida with bachelor and masters in Architecture & Interior Design, we both worked in very well known local architecture firms like Arquitectonica, MSA Architects among others. Our studio emphasis design by the use of materiality and the necessity of the community and user to enrich interior and exterior spaces for commercial and residential market. Most recent work in our portfolio will be Little Haiti Station, Doral City Place 7 Apartments, Collins Park Garage and Lockhart Beckham Stadium. For the first time we are working on an urban planning project with “The Friends of the Underline” design public spaces that will engage the community to open spaces in Brickell and it will open during Art Basel week.
